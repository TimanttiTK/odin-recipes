Pizza

Description

Homemade Pizza that taste just like in Italy

Ingredients

  • Ham
  • 1 pack of pizza dough
  • 1 paprika
  • 1 onion
  • 400 ml of tomato sauce
  • 100 g of grated mozzarella

Steps

  1. Heat up the oven
  2. Chop the vegetables and mix them with the meat
  3. Throw everything on the pizza dough
  4. Bake at 250 degrees for 20 minutes

Lasagne

Description

Lasagne in Mamas way!

Ingredients

  • 400 g of minced meat
  • 1 pack of lasagne sheets
  • 1 L of milk
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 400 ml of tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Heat up the oven
  2. Shop the onions and carrots
  3. Assemble everything on the tray
  4. Let it bake for 2 hours

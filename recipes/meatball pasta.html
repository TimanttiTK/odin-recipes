Meatball pasta

Description

A delicious meatball pasta straight from Sweden

Ingredients

  • 400 g of minced meat
  • 1 pack spaghetti
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 onion
  • 400 ml of tomato sauce
  • 100 g of grated mozzarella

Steps

  1. Heat up the pan
  2. Roll the minced meat into balls
  3. Chop the vegetables and mix them with the meat
  4. Cook the balls on the pan
  5. Cook the spaghetti 8 minutes in boiling water
